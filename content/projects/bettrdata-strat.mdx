Internal strategy work, run as a project rather than a deck. I spent six months pulling apart how three incumbent platforms were winning, where their pricing was load-bearing, and where the wedge for a focused operations platform actually was. The output was a repositioning, a new pricing model, and a quarter of GTM tests that gave us the answer.

Most of the work was unglamorous: thirty customer interviews, a competitive teardown that ran to forty pages, and a pricing experiment that cost us two deals before it started winning them. Strategy is a verb.

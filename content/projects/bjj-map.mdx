A node-link graph of BJJ positions and the techniques that connect them. Built originally as a way to organize my own notes; it now gets a few hundred unique visitors a month from search.

It runs as a static site with a tiny graph viewer I wrote in vanilla JS — no React, no framework, no bundler. The whole thing weighs less than a single Next.js page. Sometimes the right stack is none.

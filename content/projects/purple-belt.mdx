Twelve months of deliberate progression, organized into three four-month blocks: bottom game, top game, and submissions. Each block has a single technical focus and a small list of techniques I'm trying to take from conscious to unconscious.

I run it like a roadmap. One big bet, two supporting bets, and a column for whatever shows up in rolling that I want to chase. So far this year the surprise column has been leg entanglements — I'd been avoiding them and they've quietly become the thing I'm best at.

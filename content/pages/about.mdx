# About

I'm a product leader and builder. Right now, that means I'm CPO at BettrData, where I lead product, design, and engineering. I'm also solo-building Syscribe—an AI-powered documentation platform. I'm based in Auckland, having recently moved here from Boulder.

Outside of that, I spend most of my time pursuing athletic endeavors, reading, and building other projects.

I'm deeply interested in process—not in the org-chart sense, but in how people actually do things. Intentional focus on *how* you do things is the best leverage people, teams, and organizations have, and the nuance and complexity is where the interest lives.

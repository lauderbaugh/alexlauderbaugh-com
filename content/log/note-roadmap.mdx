---
title: "On roadmaps that survive the quarter"
date: 2026-04-22
type: note
draft: false
---

Every roadmap I've shipped that held up had the same shape: one big bet, two supporting bets, and a deliberate empty column for whatever the market does to us. The empty column is the trick. If you fill it in January you have nowhere to put February's surprise.

The teams that hate this usually want certainty more than they want correctness. Worth saying out loud.
